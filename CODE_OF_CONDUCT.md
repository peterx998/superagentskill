# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project welcomes practical, respectful contributions. Keep discussion focused on improving the skills library, routing quality, documentation, and safety.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and constructive.
- Respect different experience levels and language backgrounds.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep examples free of private data.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing changes carefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing someone else's private information
- Introducing malicious code, unsafe instructions, or secret material
- Repeated off-topic disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to being blocked from participating.
