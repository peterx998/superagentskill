## Processing Checklist

- Does the note have a clear `type` and `status`?
- Is the title specific enough to find later?
- Are source facts separated from personal interpretation?
- Are there useful `[[wiki links]]`?
- Are tags consistent with the vault taxonomy?
- Can any idea become an evergreen note?
- Does the note connect to at least one MOC or project?
